Amy Amatya

I am a California resident, a Research & Communications Fellow at a climate news nonprofit, and a recent Princeton grad where I studied earth science and anthropology. I am broadly interested in what subaltern studies and critical race theory can tell us about how environmental ruin operates, and places itself in, the precariat.
